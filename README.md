# rythm-tap

Rhythm Tap

A browser-based rhythm game where bubbles fall down the screen and you have to hit them when they reach the target circle. The game lasts 30 seconds and gives you a letter grade at the end based on your accuracy.

How To Play
 Select a difficulty on the start screen and press play. Hit spacebar or tap anywhere on the screen when a bubble reaches the circle. The closer your timing is to the exact moment the bubble lands, the better your rating.

Combo and Scoring
Every hit adds to your combo. Hitting 5 in a row gives you a x2 multiplier, 10 gives x3, and 20 gives x4. Missing resets the combo back to zero. Perfects are worth 100 points and goods are worth 50, both multiplied by your current combo.
